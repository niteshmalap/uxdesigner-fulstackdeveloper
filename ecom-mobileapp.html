E-commerce Mobile App: Bridging Gaps and Boosting Sales

Collaborated with a prominent American retail chain encompassing 48 stores, to create an enriched mobile app experience.

Problem statement

They lacked a mobile app, adversely affecting sales among a specific age group of users. Given the contemporary need for e-commerce mobile apps, this absence was evident and impactful.

The Challenge

Create a mobile app that offers a delightful user experience across all age groups, resulting in enhanced conversion rates, reduced support calls, and increased sales.

Research

1. Conducting competitor analysis provides valuable insights into integrating the latest features into the app.

2. Stakeholder interviews align the entire team towards a common direction and provide success metrics for the mobile app.

3. User research exposed pain points across various aspects of the website experience, offering guidance for enhancing accessibility across all age groups.

Ideate

Based on user research, we pinpointed challenges, prioritized them, and began generating solutions. Collaborating with the tech team, we refined the solution, crafted low-fidelity wireframe prototypes, and conducted user testing

Prototype and Test

Following feedback on the low-fidelity wireframes, we made enhancements and presented high-fidelity design prototypes for testing. This step had a significant impact on refining our designs and engineering solutions for the mobile app.
