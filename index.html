Summary

With 17+ years' expertise in UX/UI design, I excel in crafting engaging user experiences, leading teams to achieve a 40% surge in user engagement for e-commerce and financial platforms. Adept in Figma, InVision, Sketch, and Adobe Creative Suite, I deliver innovative designs meeting client expectations. Strong communication and problem-solving skills ensure on-time, budget-friendly project delivery.

30% of my time is devoted to comprehending requirements and defining problems, encompassing assumptions, feature listings, and crafting user stories. In the realm of product design, I develop an Opportunity Solution Tree.

Following this, 40% of my time is allocated to ideating solutions for these narratives. This phase involves sketching, whiteboarding, wireframing, and testing wireframes via clickable prototypes. Subsequently, I dedicate 30% of my time to visual designs, encompassing the design system and interaction design.
